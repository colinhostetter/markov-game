Markov's Revenge

Can you guess which sentence is being written by a human, and which is being written by a computer?

It works like this:

  • You'll play with a partner. Each round, one of you is the writer, and the other is the guesser.
  • The writer will be given the beginning of a sentence and have to complete it by choosing words one at a time. Meanwhile, the computer will randomly generate two other sentences.
  • Once the sentences are written, the guesser will have to guess which of the three sentences was written by the other player.
  • Then, you'll swap roles!
